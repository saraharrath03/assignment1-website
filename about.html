FAQ

How long until results?

Progress varies—evaluate trends over 4–8 weeks along with habits (protein intake, steps, sleep).

Can I take Hers with coffee?

Generally yes. If sensitive to caffeine, take with food and monitor how you feel; consult a clinician if unsure.

Is it safe for everyone?

No supplement is right for everyone. Check the label and speak with a healthcare professional when needed.
